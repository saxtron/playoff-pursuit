Playoff Pursuit

Pick any 10 players. A point is awarded for every GOAL, ASSIST, and GAME-WINNING GOAL. The deadline to submit picks is Wednesday April 13th at 7pm. More Rules

Instructions

Choose any 10 players to create your fantasy playoff team! The top 10 scoring leaders on each team are listed. A player shown in red may be injured; click on his name for more information. If you wish to choose a player not listed, please fill in his name under the Additional Players heading.

Scoring

A point is awarded for a goal, an assist, and a game-winning goal. (If a player you selected gets a game-winning goal, you will score 2 points total. One for a goal, one for a game-winning goal)

Entry Fee

The entry fee to the pool is $10. Please coordinate with Mike Saxton, Scott Gutcher, or Matt White to make your payment. The deadline for payment is Wednesday April 20th; if payment is not received your entry is invalid. If you would like to pay through the post or by email money transfer please contact us.

Prizes

To be determined based on number of participants. There will be a prize for the top three finishers at least. 100% of entry fees will go towards prizes. (Last year's prizes were: 1st: $500, 2nd: $250, 3rd: $150, 4th: $100, 5th: $60)

Help

If you have any questions or comments please contact us.

Tie-Breaking Procedure

Ties in the final point standings will be settled first by most total goals, and then by most total assists. If a tie persists, the teams will reamin tied, and any prizes will be split equally.
